Welcome to Revision

Revision leverages Google Docs to store and manage your work

This helps ensure that you retain full control and ownership of your writing, and you're able to control who can collaborate on your work with you.

Click the "Sign In" button above to get started.

What is "Revision"?

Great question! Jon should fill this in more later, but the short answer is that it's a serverless web tool for screenwriters to write and collaborate. We don't store any of your data, we only interact with Google Docs on your behalf to store your changes and updates.
